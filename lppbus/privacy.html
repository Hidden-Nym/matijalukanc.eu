Privacy Policy – LPPBus

Last updated: March 25, 2026

Overview

LPPBus ("the App") is developed by Matija Lukanc. This Privacy Policy explains how the App handles your information.

Data Collection

The App does not collect, store, or share any personal data. No user accounts are required and no personally identifiable information is gathered.

Analytics & Tracking

The App does not use any analytics services, advertising frameworks, or tracking technologies.

Third-Party Services

The App fetches real-time bus arrival data from public LPP (Ljubljanski potniški promet) APIs. These requests do not include any personal information. Please refer to LPP's own privacy policy for information about how they handle their API traffic.

Data Storage

All app preferences and data are stored locally on your device and are not transmitted to any external server.

Children's Privacy

The App does not knowingly collect any data from children or any other users.

Changes to This Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated date.

Contact

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact me at lukancmatija@icloud.com.
